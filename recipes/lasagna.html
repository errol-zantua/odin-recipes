Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of my favorite pasta dishes created.

I even more like it if it's stuffed with alot of cheese and meat! Yum!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 blocks cheese, grated
  • 6 pcs lasagna noodles
  • 1 pc large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, choped
  • 1/2 lbs ground beef
  • 500g spaghetti sauce
  • 250g tomato sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 5 tbsp butter
  • 4 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 pc egg

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F. Brown ground meat in a large skillet on medium heat and stir occasionally. Mix spaghetti sauce into meat mixture
  2. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce onto the bottom of a small to medium-sized baking dish and top with lasagna noodles until the layer is full
  3. Spread half of the cheese mixture over the noodles
  4. Repeat meat sauce, noodles, and cheese layers once. Top with remaining noodles and meat sauce. Make sure to cover noodles with sauce. Sprinkle the remaning 1/2 cup of cheese on top. Cover with foil
  5. Bake for 45 minutes. Remove foil. Bake for 15 minutes or until noodles are tender and cheese is golden brown. Let stand for another 15 minutes before cutting
